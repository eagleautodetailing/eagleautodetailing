This site uses Netlify Forms. To receive each submission by EMAIL:

1) Deploy this folder to Netlify (Netlify Drop or from Git).
2) In your Netlify dashboard: Site -> Forms -> 'contact' -> Notifications.
3) Click 'Add notification' -> 'Email' -> enter your email address -> Save.

Optional (recommended):
- Enable spam protection: in the same Form settings, turn on reCAPTCHA or Akismet.
- Export submissions: Forms -> contact -> Export CSV.

That's it! Every new submission will be emailed to you.
